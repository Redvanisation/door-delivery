about us

Door Delivery is a platform that was established just last night in this Hackattack which lets the elderly or disabled people to order groceries from their local market at free of cost. We are a non-profit community serving platform. We do not have any locations as we completely operate online at the moment. If you would like to support us in anyway, You can also use the Volunteer page on our site to leave your info. We will contact you shortly!!

Our vision

Door Delivery is a start up and we wish to help local communities especially among the elders and disabled. But we do not want to be limited on just that. We want to expand in future and serve the community among all people and just specific groups.
